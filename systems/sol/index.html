Solar abundance page in preparation

This page will present the full solar reference dataset used in all Codex measurements — photospheric abundances for 25 elements from Asplund et al. (2021), with NLTE corrections and comparison to Lodders (2003) and Grevesse & Sauval (1998).

The Sun is not a target — it’s the ruler. Every abundance in the Codex is expressed as [X/H] = log(Nₓ/N₂)★ − log(Nₓ/N₂)☉, which means getting the solar reference right is the single most important calibration decision in the entire pipeline.
