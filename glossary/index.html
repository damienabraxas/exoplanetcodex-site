[G] ○ In preparation — RYA-73

Glossary in preparation

The Codex Glossary will cover ~120 terms across stellar spectroscopy, LTE line formation, equivalent width analysis, stellar atmosphere modeling, and exoplanet interior structure. A subset of key terms:

Equivalent widthThe width of a rectangular strip of the continuum that has the same total absorption as an observed spectral line. Measured in mÅ or pm.
[Fe/H]Iron abundance relative to solar, in dex. [Fe/H] = log(N₟ₑ/N₂)★ − log(N₟ₑ/N₂)☉. The primary metallicity indicator.
C/O ratioThe ratio of carbon to oxygen number densities in the stellar photosphere. A C/O > 0.8 has implications for rocky planet mineralogy.
Excitation equilibriumThe condition where Fe I abundances show no trend with excitation potential. Used to constrain Tₛₑₑ.
